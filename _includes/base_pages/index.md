**Promenadorquestern och med Baletten Paletten** (PQ) at KTH Royal Institute of Technology is the THS Student Union’s official student orchestra. We are a wind orchestra with a dance group that performs jazz, with elements of both classical and more modern styles.

The association is recognizable by their black and yellow appearance with black tailcoats, which are typical for student life at KTH.

We have shows year round, mostly for companies or at festivals. We perform both with the whole orchestra and dance group, as well as in smaller ensembles that are more appropriate for smaller events. If you interested in booking PQ for your event, please refer to the [booking page]({{ site.dir }}book-us) and contact our [Directeur](mailto:dirren@pq.se).
